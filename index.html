Brocula is a Role Playing Game about a vampire who has been asleep for 500 years. He is suddenly awaken when a car crashes into his castle. He has been living for ever, but in modern times, his money is useless. He has no food. And his castle is crumbling.

You play as Brocula in this game. You can roam around the town, talk to people, apply for jobs, farm vegetables, learn new skills, etc. Over 40 hours of gameplay.
